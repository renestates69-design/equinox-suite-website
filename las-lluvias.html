Las Lluvias de Ibiza

Agua del Cielo

Water from the Sky

A sustainable vision for Ibiza

Ibiza faces a growing water crisis. Aquifers are depleting, desalination is energy-intensive, and demand continues to rise. Las Lluvias de Ibiza proposes a decentralised rainwater harvesting infrastructure — capturing, filtering, and distributing rainwater at the estate and municipal level.

This is not a concept paper. It is a complete business plan with engineering specifications, regulatory pathway, financial projections, and implementation timeline.
