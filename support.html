Common questions

How does it work?
Type a word, mood, or concept into the search field. The Fontologist analyses your input and returns five Google Font recommendations that match the character of your idea.

Are the fonts free to use?
Yes. All recommended fonts are from Google Fonts and are available under open-source licenses for personal and commercial use.

Why do I need an API key?
The Fontologist uses AI to analyse your input. During the beta period, you provide your own Anthropic API key. This will change in the full release.

My API key isn't working.
Make sure your key starts with sk-ant- and that you have active credit on your Anthropic account.
