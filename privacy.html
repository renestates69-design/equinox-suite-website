Privacy Policy

The Fontologist by Equinox Design

What we collect

The Fontologist processes the words and phrases you type into the app to generate font recommendations. This input is sent to a third-party AI service (Anthropic Claude API) for processing and is not stored by us after your session ends.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect personal information, email addresses, or account details.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not store your search queries or font recommendations.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising.

Third-party services

Font recommendations are generated via the Anthropic Claude API. Your input text is sent to Anthropic for processing. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Recommended fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Google may collect usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

If you enter an API key, it is stored only in your browser's session storage and is cleared when you close the tab. We never transmit or store your API key on our servers.

Children's privacy

The Fontologist is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
